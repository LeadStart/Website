About LeadStart

Outbound that actually works for B2B companies.

We built LeadStart because B2B companies deserved a better answer than paid ads, lead brokers, and referral-only growth.

Most B2B companies hit a ceiling when referrals slow down — not because the business isn't good, but because nobody's told them what comes next. LeadStart is what comes next.
— Daniel, Founder

Why this exists.

B2B services are incredible businesses. Recurring revenue, sticky contracts, protectable margins. But the growth playbook most owners are handed — Google Ads, bid listings, and hoping referrals keep coming — quietly stops working somewhere around $1M–$5M in revenue.

That's the moment most B2B companies start to plateau. Not because demand dried up, but because nobody ever taught them how to do outbound. And the "marketing agencies" who promise they can? They've never run a real outbound campaign in your space. They'll sell you generic lead-gen and hand you a list that's been sold to your three biggest competitors.

LeadStart is what happens when you build an outbound agency that does only one thing: book qualified intro calls with your target buyers, with territory exclusivity and honest pricing.

Who we work with

Built for B2B companies with capacity to grow.

Commercial-focused

B2B services, SaaS, professional services, agencies. If your bread and butter is long-sales-cycle B2B contracts, you're our fit. We don't work on B2C.

Capacity to service

You have the crews, supervisors, and ops to onboard new accounts. We're building pipeline — you need the ability to actually close and service what we put in front of you.

Ready to invest in growth

Outbound is a compound investment. The first month is ramp, the second is real momentum. If you're looking for instant leads at no cost, we're not the right fit — and we'll tell you that on the intro call.

What makes us different.

We only do B2B outbound.

No chasing every shiny new channel. Every copy pattern, every list source, every reply we've seen — it's all been refined through years of B2B outbound. That specialization compounds over time.

One client per territory. Full stop.

Lead brokers make money by selling the same list six times. We make money when your one exclusive campaign actually produces. Completely different incentive structure, completely different outcomes.

You own everything we build.

Your prospect list, your inboxes, your copy, your sequences. If you ever want to run outbound in-house, we'll hand you the full playbook. Most clients stay because it's easier to let us run it — but the choice is always yours.
