Billing FAQ

Pricing questions, answered.

Is there a minimum contract length?

No. Every plan is month-to-month. You can cancel at any time with no penalty. If the campaign isn't producing, we'd rather refund and part ways than keep you locked in.

How does the money-back guarantee work in practice?

On your intro call we agree to a specific qualified-lead target for your market. If we don't hit that target within the agreed timeframe, we refund 50% of your setup fee and continue running your campaigns free until we do. You're never paying for a service that isn't producing.

When is the monthly fee billed?

Monthly fee starts the day your first send goes out — not on the day you sign. Setup weeks aren't counted against you. You're only paying once the outbound engine is actually running.

Can I upgrade or downgrade between plans?

Yes, any time. Most clients start on Starter or Growth and move up as their capacity grows. Downgrades take effect the next billing cycle; upgrades can take effect immediately if your infrastructure supports the volume.

Do you offer custom plans above Scale?

Yes. If you need multiple metros, higher volume, or custom reporting, we'll build a plan that fits. Reach out via the contact page or call Daniel directly at 425-286-9732.

Ready to get a quote for your market?

Book a 20-minute call. We'll size your territory, agree on a lead target, and quote the setup transparently.
