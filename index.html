FAQ

Questions we hear most.

Straight answers on lead quality, guarantees, territory, and what's actually happening behind the scenes.

How quickly will I see results?

Setup and inbox warming takes the first two weeks. Sends begin in week 3 at a conservative volume and ramp up over the first month. Most clients see their first booked intro calls between weeks 3 and 5, with volume increasing through month two as the full sequence rolls out.

What makes your lists different from ones I've bought before?

Recycled lists get everyone nowhere. Ours are pulled fresh for your exact market and role targets, then double-verified before a single send. You own every contact outright — nothing is resold, and nothing overlaps with another client's outreach.

What happens if I don't hit my lead target?

We refund 50% of your setup fee and keep working free until you hit the agreed target. We don't grow if our clients don't grow — the guarantee forces us to pick markets and service fits we can actually deliver on.

How is territory exclusivity enforced?

We sign one client per territory per vertical. Your prospect list is carved from that market and reserved for you — no one else we work with is emailing your prospects, period. If a direct competitor approaches us for the same territory, we decline.

Do I need to do anything technical?

No. We handle domains, inbox configuration, deliverability, sequence setup, warming, and follow-ups. Your only job is replying to interested leads — which we route to your main inbox in real time.

What does the one-time setup fee cover?

Domain and inbox setup, deliverability warming, custom copy development, initial prospect list build, and verification. The fee scales with how large your addressable market is — the bigger your territory, the more contacts we validate up front. We price it transparently on your intro call.

Can I cancel whenever?

Yes. Month-to-month. No annual contracts. If the campaign isn't producing, we'd rather refund you and part ways than have you locked in.

Who is actually doing the work?

A small, direct team — no outsourcing, no offshore content mills. Daniel runs strategy and client calls. Copy and list builds are handled in-house by people who've worked on B2B outbound specifically.
